Pay Per Result Service Agreement

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Peak Leads Group ("PLG") and the client ("Client"). By submitting payment for the onboarding invoice, the Client acknowledges and agrees to the terms set forth in this Agreement, and Peak Leads Group agrees to uphold its responsibilities as outlined herein. Payment constitutes mutual acceptance of this Agreement, including the services, responsibilities, and billing terms described below.

1
Services Provided

PLG will deliver qualified homeowner leads through targeted Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns.

2
Partnership Structure

Onboarding Package – $1980 (due upon signing)

This covers your full campaign setup and the first 15 qualified leads.

Ongoing Billing – $99 per lead

After the initial 15 leads, you'll be billed $99 per lead, charged automatically in increments of 5 leads using the payment method on file. By entering and maintaining a valid payment method, the Client authorizes PLG to charge this method automatically in accordance with this Agreement.

3
Qualified Leads

A Qualified Lead meets all of the following:

  • Verified homeowner – as determined by the onboarding intake form on the landing page
  • Zip code provided is within the agreed-upon service area
  • Includes a phone number that is working and in service
  • Includes a valid email address, as determined by an online email verification tool

If a lead does not meet these criteria, it can be reported to PLG within 7 days of delivery for review. If confirmed, it will be replaced free of charge.

4
Lead Delivery & Communication

Qualified leads will be delivered to the client's chosen contact method. It is the client's responsibility to respond promptly to leads to ensure successful outcomes.

5
Guarantee

If PLG fails to deliver the initial 15 qualified leads within 60 days of campaign launch, the client can receive a refund for the remaining undelivered leads if they so choose.

6
Cancellation Policy

Either party may cancel this agreement at any time for any reason, provided the Client pays any outstanding balance owed for leads delivered up to the date of cancellation. Upon cancellation, lead delivery will cease within 24 hours. The Client remains responsible for paying for any qualified leads that are delivered during that 24-hour period.

7
Payment Terms

Client agrees to maintain a valid payment method on file. If a charge fails or is declined, lead delivery will be immediately paused until the issue is resolved. Repeated payment failures may result in termination of this agreement.

11
